SlideSpeech
Corpus

A large scale slide-enriched audio-visual corpus

Dataset Features

Abundance of slides in videos

These can be used for text-enhanced multi-modal ASR to correct the wrong recognized proprietary terms.

Sizeable

1,705 videos with a total duration of 1,000+ hours, and 473 hours of transcribed speech.

Diverse

Adiverse range of domain categories, with 22 classes.
